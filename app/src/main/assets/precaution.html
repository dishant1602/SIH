Precaution



  • The chardham are accessible only for six months (May to October) throughout the year. The region becomes inaccessible during the other half of the year due to snowfall. You can check the opening and closing dates of Char Dham Temples in the chardham section in our app.

  • Perform Biometric Registration in advance before visiting specific char dham and carry ID proofs like Aadhar card/visa/passport/driving license with you.

  • While visiting Hindu mythological sites, shrines and temples, one should remove shoes and cover the head with a piece of cloth before entering the Holy Place.

  • Carry heavy woolen clothes to protect from cold weather during the month of October-November and carry moderate woolen during Summer Season.

  • Carry a woolen Blanket, an umbrella, Raincoat, Torchlight, minimum luggage and canvas shoes with you. You can carry a woolen sweater, monkey cap and muffler, which will enable you to withstand cold winds, at the upper level of the Himalayas.

  • Do carry creams, moisturizers, sunscreen creams.

  • Pack a medical kit with painkillers, antibiotics, cough lozenges, Antiseptic cream, Iodine, tube-squeeze cream and medicines for cold and fever.

  • Carry your routine medicines, as you might find only limited and general medicine at such remote locations. Like, Diabetic, Blood Pressure Medicines

  • Carry dark chocolates, dry fruits, Glucose (Glucon D), toffees. They will provide your instant energy during the trekking and during long jams.

  • Do not take pictures if cameras are not allowed in any particular area.

  • Do not play with the sentiments of any particular religion.

  • Carry extra batteries and films for your camera, as electricity is not regular in such remote places.

  • Do not travel in the rainy season as there are a lot of landslides during that time.

  • Book hotel rooms in advance if you are traveling close to the temple opening dates as there is a heavy rush of pilgrims.

  • Alcohol or non vegetarian food is not permitted during Char Dham Yatra Tour.

  • Check the weather in our app and prepare the tour accordingly.

  • If you want to climb a hill, the air will get thinner as you go up, so keep it with an oxygen cylinder.
